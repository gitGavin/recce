### Summary

Tableau Pulse became generally available in February 2024 as part of the Tableau 2024.1 release, representing a reimagined data experience that makes data more accessible to everyone regardless of expertise, available for Tableau Cloud users and leveraging Tableau AI to deliver personalized, contextual, and smart insights. The product is included free with all Tableau Cloud editions and Embedded Analytics solutions, though premium features like Cross-Metric Analysis, Enhanced Q&A, and advanced capabilities are only available in the Tableau+ Bundle. 

Tableau Pulse's competitive positioning centers on three key innovations: a Metrics Layer (headless BI) that enables metrics to be defined once as a single source of truth, an Insights platform using statistical models that automatically generate and rank insights, then translate them into natural language using generative AI, and Next-Gen Experiences delivering insights directly in users' flow of work via Slack, Teams, email, and mobile. The product continues rapid development with bi-weekly feature releases as of August 2025, and recent additions include Microsoft 365 integration for PowerPoint and Word and dashboard filtering capabilities.
